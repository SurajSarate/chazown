Chazown Private Limited has been at the forefront of Industrial Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (IEPC) since 2018, harnessing market opportunities and our internal strengths in MEP and EPC.


Over the past years, we have built extensive capabilities in engineering, design, procurement, and construction. Today, we are a leading one-stop solutions provider in the EPC segment, offering tailored solutions to our clients.

Our IEPC division undertakes comprehensive projects, including Civil, Electromechanical, Instrumentation & Controls, Security, and Firefighting & Detection works. We serve a wide range of industrial sectors such as Oil & Gas, Cryogenic, Mines, Power, Defence, Chemical, Fertilizers, Steel, and Large Infrastructure projects.

Certified by renowned PMC and EPC contractors in India, we have also expanded our footprint internationally, providing expertise in fire alarms, firefighting, low voltage, control, and instrumentation systems.Our team comprises experts across multiple verticals, capable of delivering end-to-end solutions. From design and engineering, procurement and supply, project management and planning, to erection, testing, commissioning, and project handover, we ensure HSE and quality compliance within contractual timelines.
