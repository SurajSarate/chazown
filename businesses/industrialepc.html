Chazown ventured into Industrial Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (IEPC) in 2015 to exploit the ever-growing market opportunities and leverage the company’s internal strength in MEP and EPC.


Over the last few years, the company has developed significant capabilities in multiple disciplines like engineering, design, procurement, and construction. Today, we are the one-stop solutions provider in the EPC segment offering customised solutions to our clients.

The IEPC business division undertakes Civil, Electromechanical, Instrumentation & Controls, Security, Firefighting & Detection works. We cater to a large section of industrial projects in Oil & Gas, Cryogenic, Mines, Power, Defence, Chemical, Fertilizers, Steel, Manufacturing Plants, Large Infrastructure, etc.

The company is certified by all renowned PMC and EPC contractors in India. It has entered the international market by providing solutions in fire alarms, fire fighting, low voltage, control, and instrumentation systems.

Our team comprises experts across multiple verticals who have the capability of providing end-to-end solutions, starting from design and engineering, procurement and supply, project management and planning, erection, testing, commissioning, and handing over the project to the end-user ensuring HSE and quality compliance, within the contractual timelines.
