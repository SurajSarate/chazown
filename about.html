Welcome to Chazown Private Limited (pronounced khaw-ZONE), a name derived from the Hebrew word meaning a dream, revelation, or vision.
Founded as a company specializing in MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) contracting, we operate across India, delivering high-quality services for several high-profile projects. Chazown Pvt Ltd is dedicated to providing comprehensive Mechanical, Plumbing, and Firefighting services to residential, commercial, and infrastructure sectors.

Transitioning from calling ourselves contractors to establishing our identity as an engineering company marks a significant evolution in our journey. We believe in the power of value engineering. Our approach goes beyond traditional contracting; we meticulously analyze and optimize every aspect of our projects to deliver superior outcomes.
